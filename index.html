About

Agriculture is one of the most important aspects of everyone's life.it is necesssary for the survival of every human being Along with being a necessary, it also helps the country's economy.

Meaning

Agriculture has been derived from two different latin words,'ager' And 'cultra' where ager means 'field' and cultra means 'growing' or cultivation.So the literal meaning of agriculture is the 'cultivation of fields'.

Definition

Agriculture is the practice of farming ,including cultivating the soil for the growing crops,rearing animals and producing other products such as wool,oil,etc.

Agriculture plays a very vital role in our life.Without agriculture,the existance of human beings is impossible as it is the primary source of our food supply to sustain on the earth,and it also helps to grow our economy across the world.
